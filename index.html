About Us

We are a team of very devoted developers. What matters the most to us is our customers' experience on MoiCart to be the best possible.
It obviously means that we are open to changes, and that your thoughts are important to us. We want you to ask for new features, we'll always try to figure it out for you, and this way, MoiCart will keep on improving.
As we're keen to answer to our beloved customers, you can browse to our contact page if you want to approach us.

As we said, our goal is to help you save money, not to make you pay: this means that our app is free.
We still have a pro version with very little fees which gets you a lot of advantages.
