About Us

moicart is a service dedicated to helping you find nearby stores that have your groceries at the cheapest prices. By using our app and connecting you with thousands of stores you can be confident that you are getting the best deals.

Make your chores a little bit
easier with moicart.

Features

Product Features

How the app works is simple - input your grocery list and search radius then press Go. That's it! The app will use keywords from your list and search for the cheapest price. It then ranks the stores based on the amount of items that are the cheapest at that store. We also offer delivery services - allowing you to save as much time as possible during your busy day.
